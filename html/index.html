About us

With more than 30 years of experience, Cheshmehsar Foundry has been creating specialty cast iron products. It is a modern and innovative facility with 130,000 square meters of manufacturing space, located in Zanjan, Iran. We have the capacity to produce 10,000 tons per year using our automatic high volume molding lines. In addition to state-of-the-art NDT equipments, we are equipped with complete mechanical testing and metallography labs to ensure that we meet design specifications and comply with ISO/TS 16949. We strive for innovation and efficiency in the product development by utilizing computer aided pattern design (CAD) and simulation. We are involved in our community by supporting various organizations: Mehraneh, Roozbeh and Ra'd charities. We are also committed to youth education by creating coop placements with mentorship.

We pride ourselves in the quality of our products and the way we do business. We strongly believe in customer care and our long standing relationship with clients can attest to this approach.

Molding Machine

Our automated molding machines provide high quality castings with unparalleled consistency in the finished product. We have DISA automatic machine along with AGM to accommodate different sizes and quantities of castings per customer specifications. These molding lines are capable of handling castings ranging from Grams up to 50 Kgs

DISAMATIC (2013 MK5B)
In 2008, Cheshmehsar Foundry introduced a new addition to their principal equipment, the DISA. By combining vertical and flaskless horizontal molding technologies, the Disamatic offers high precision, speed and quality. Vertical production technology was first introduced in 1964, and then it was developed by DISA Industries A/S. DISA Company is a world leader in the sphere of foundry casting equipment, foundry sand preparation systems, and mold core production equipment. DISA molding lines have a great working stability they are compact and easy to operate. Majority of safety measures and protection devices protect operator on each working phase.

Melting

Melting furnaces used in the foundry industry are of many diverse configurations. The selection of the melting unit is one of the most important decisions foundries must make with consideration to several important factors.

In our leading-edge melting shop, materials are made individually for our customers. Next to plain grades conforming to all standards, our standard range covers some 30 different alloys that can be made according to specific requirement profiles.

The raw materials on which our alloys are based include pig iron, steel scrap, foundry scrap, and recycled material. Effective quality assurance necessarily includes inspecting raw materials as they arrive. Furthermore, we continuously inspect melts by thermal and spectral analysis to ensure that their composition matches the customers' specifications.
